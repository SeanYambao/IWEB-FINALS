HALO-HALO

Halo-Halo means "mix-mix," a colorful and refreshing Filipino dessert. It's made of crushed ice, milk, sweetened beans, fruits, jellies, leche flan, and topped with ube ice cream. It's the ultimate summer treat!

PREP TIME
15 mins
COOK TIME
0 mins
SERVINGS
1
DIFFICULTY
Very Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cupshaved ice
1/4 cupsweetened bananas
1/4 cupsweetened jackfruit
1/4 cupsweetened beans
1/4 cupnata de coco or gulaman (jelly)
1/4 cupleche flan (sliced)
1 scoopube ice cream
to drizzleevaporated milk

How to Make

  1. In a tall glass, layer sweetened fruits and beans at the bottom.
  2. Add shaved ice on top.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Top with leche flan and ube ice cream.
  5. Mix well before eating!
