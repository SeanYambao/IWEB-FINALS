BULALO

Bulalo is a comforting beef shank soup with bone marrow, simmered for hours until the meat is tender and the broth is rich. It's usually paired with corn, cabbage, and potatoes, making it hearty and filling. Best enjoyed steaming hot on a cool day.

PREP TIME
20 mins
COOK TIME
3 hours
SERVINGS
6-8
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 kgbeef shank with bone marrow
1onion, quartered
1 tbsppeppercorns
2 tbspfish sauce
2corn on the cob, cut into pieces
2potatoes, quartered
1 bunchpechay or cabbage
8 cupswater

How to Cook

  1. Boil beef shank in water, skim off scum.
  2. Add onion, peppercorns, and fish sauce. Simmer for 2–3 hours until meat is tender.
  3. Add corn and potatoes, simmer until cooked.
  4. Add pechay or cabbage last, cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with rice and patis + calamansi dip.
