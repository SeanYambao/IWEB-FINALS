CALDERETA

Caldereta is a hearty tomato-based beef stew, usually made with beef or goat and liver spread for richness. It's a festive dish often served at gatherings, with a savory, slightly sweet, and mildly spicy flavor.

PREP TIME
30 mins
COOK TIME
2 hours
SERVINGS
6-8
DIFFICULTY
Medium

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 kgbeef (short ribs or stew cut), cubed
1onion, chopped
3 clovesgarlic, minced
1 cuptomato sauce
1/4 cupliver spread
2potatoes, cubed
2carrots, sliced
1red bell pepper, sliced
1green bell pepper, sliced
2 tbspsoy sauce
1–2 cupsbeef broth
2 tbspoil
to tastesalt, pepper, and chili flakes

How to Cook

  1. Sauté garlic and onion in oil. Add beef and brown on all sides.
  2. Add soy sauce and tomato sauce. Stir well.
  3. Pour in broth and simmer until beef is tender (1–2 hours).
  4. Add liver spread and mix until sauce thickens.
  5. Add potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  6. Adjust seasoning. Serve hot with rice.
