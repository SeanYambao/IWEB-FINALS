BICOL EXPRESS

Bicol Express is a spicy pork dish from the Bicol region, where coconut milk and chili are staple ingredients. It's creamy, fiery, and savory, making it one of the most beloved spicy Filipino dishes.

PREP TIME
20 mins
COOK TIME
45 mins
SERVINGS
4-6
DIFFICULTY
Medium

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
500 gpork belly, sliced thin
2 cupscoconut milk
1 cupcoconut cream
1onion, chopped
4 clovesgarlic, minced
5–6green chilies (siling haba), sliced
2–3bird's eye chilies, chopped (optional)
2 tbspshrimp paste (bagoong alamang)
to tastesalt and pepper

How to Cook

  1. In a pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. Add pork belly and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Stir in shrimp paste, then pour in coconut milk. Simmer until pork is tender.
  4. Add chilies and coconut cream. Simmer until thick and creamy.
  5. Season to taste. Serve with steamed rice.
