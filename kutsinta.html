PUTO & KUTSINTA

Kutsinta are steamed rice cakes with a chewy texture and beautiful amber color. They are a popular Filipino snack often paired with puto and served with grated coconut.

PREP TIME
15 mins
COOK TIME
40 mins
SERVINGS
6-8
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cuprice flour
1 cupbrown sugar
1/2 cupcassava flour
2 cupswater
1 tsplye water
for servinggrated coconut

How to Cook (Kutsinta)

  1. Mix all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into molds. Steam for 30–40 minutes until set.
  3. Serve with grated coconut.
