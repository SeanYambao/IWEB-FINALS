LUMPIA

Lumpia are Filipino spring rolls, either fried (lumpiang shanghai) filled with meat and vegetables, or fresh (lumpiang sariwa) wrapped in soft crepes with peanut sauce. Lumpiang shanghai is a party favorite and often disappears fast from the table!

PREP TIME
30 mins
COOK TIME
15 mins
SERVINGS
4-6
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
500 gground pork
1carrot, grated
1onion, minced
3 clovesgarlic, minced
1egg
2 tbspsoy sauce
to tastesalt and pepper
as neededlumpia wrappers
for fryingoil

How to Cook

  1. Mix pork, carrot, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
  2. Place 1 tbsp of filling on lumpia wrapper, roll tightly, seal with water.
  3. Heat oil, deep-fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve with sweet chili sauce or ketchup.
