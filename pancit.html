PANCIT

Pancit is a staple in Filipino celebrations, especially birthdays, symbolizing long life. Pancit bihon is made of rice noodles stir-fried with meat, seafood, and vegetables — a quick, flavorful, and filling dish.

PREP TIME
20 mins
COOK TIME
15 mins
SERVINGS
4-6
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
400 gbihon (rice noodles), soaked
200 gchicken (shredded) or pork slices
200 gshrimp (optional)
1onion, sliced
3 clovesgarlic, minced
2 tbspsoy sauce
1 tbspfish sauce
2 cupschicken broth
as neededvegetables: carrots, cabbage, bell peppers, green beans
for servingcalamansi or lemon wedges

How to Cook

  1. Sauté garlic and onion in oil. Add chicken/pork and cook until browned.
  2. Add shrimp, soy sauce, and fish sauce. Stir well.
  3. Pour chicken broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add vegetables and simmer until half-cooked.
  5. Add bihon noodles, stir until liquid is absorbed and noodles are tender.
  6. Serve hot with calamansi.
