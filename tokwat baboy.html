TOKWA'T BABOY

Tokwa't Baboy is a simple yet flavorful dish of boiled pork and fried tofu served with a tangy soy-vinegar sauce. It's often eaten as an appetizer or pulutan with drinks.

PREP TIME
20 mins
COOK TIME
30 mins
SERVINGS
4-6
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
300 gpork belly or ears, boiled and sliced
300 gtofu, fried and cubed
1onion, sliced
1/2 cupsoy sauce
1/3 cupvinegar
2 clovesgarlic, minced
1chili, chopped (optional)
to tastepepper

How to Cook

  1. Boil pork until tender, slice into bite-size pieces.
  2. Fry tofu until golden, cut into cubes.
  3. Mix soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, onion, chili, and pepper to make sauce.
  4. Combine pork, tofu, and sauce in a bowl. Serve.
