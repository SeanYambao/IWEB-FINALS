BIBINGKA

Bibingka is a soft rice cake traditionally baked in clay pots lined with banana leaves. It's a classic Christmas delicacy, often enjoyed after Simbang Gabi (midnight mass).

PREP TIME
20 mins
COOK TIME
25 mins
SERVINGS
6-8
DIFFICULTY
Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
2 cupsrice flour
1 cupcoconut milk
1/2 cupsugar
2 tspbaking powder
2eggs
as neededbanana leaves
optionalsalted egg slices and cheese (topping)

How to Cook

  1. Line baking pan with banana leaves.
  2. Mix rice flour, coconut milk, sugar, baking powder, and eggs into a batter.
  3. Pour into pan, top with salted egg and cheese.
  4. Bake at 180°C for 20–25 minutes or until golden.
  5. Brush with butter and sprinkle sugar before serving.
