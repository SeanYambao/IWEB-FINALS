CHAMPORADO

Champorado is a chocolate rice porridge made with sticky rice and cocoa. Sweet and comforting, it's often paired with tuyo (dried fish) for a unique salty-sweet breakfast combo.

PREP TIME
5 mins
COOK TIME
30 mins
SERVINGS
4-6
DIFFICULTY
Very Easy

Ingredients

Quantity Ingredient
1 cupglutinous rice
4 cupswater
1/2 cupcocoa powder or tablea
1/2 cupsugar
1 cupevaporated or condensed milk (for topping)

How to Cook

  1. Boil water in a pot. Add glutinous rice, cook until soft.
  2. Dissolve cocoa in a little hot water, then stir into rice.
  3. Add sugar, simmer until thick and creamy.
  4. Serve hot with a drizzle of milk. Optional: pair with tuyo.
